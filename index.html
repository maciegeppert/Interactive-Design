The World's Only Corn Palace




The Corn Palace is most commonly known as "The World's Only Corn Palace, it is located in Mitchell,
South Dakota about 2 hours from Brookings. The Corn Palace is a multi-purpose arena/facility that
is decorated with murals made out of corn and other crops. Each year the building is redesigned and constructed
and about 500,000 tourists come from around the nation each year to see the uniquely designed corn murals.
