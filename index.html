The World's Only Corn Palace


The Corn Palace is most commonly known as "The World's Only Corn Palace, it is located in Mitchell, South Dakota about 2 hours from Brookings. The Corn Palace is a multi-purpose arena/facility that is decorated with murals made out of corn and other crops. Each year the building is redesigned and constructed, the Corn Palace is a popular tourist destination, up to 500,000 people visit each year."
